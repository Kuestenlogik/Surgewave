--- layout: page title: Privacy subtitle: What this site stores, what it does not. description: Privacy statement for the Surgewave marketing site. --- ## What this site stores This marketing site is statically generated and served from GitHub Pages. It does not run server-side code and does not set cookies of its own. Your browser may cache the JavaScript theme-preference in `localStorage` (key `theme`) so dark/light mode sticks between visits; clearing site data in your browser removes it. ## Third-party services - **GitHub Pages** hosts the static output. GitHub's privacy policy applies to the underlying network connection. - **Google Fonts** serves the Inter and JetBrains Mono webfonts. Requests carry your IP address to Google as part of loading the fonts. ## What this site does not do - No analytics. - No tracking pixels. - No advertising networks. - No form submissions. If the site grows a newsletter, contact form, or license shop later, this page will be updated before those features go live.
