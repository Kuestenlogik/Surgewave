{%- comment -%} Renders a screenshot as a paired dark/light image. One stays visible at a time depending on ``. Usage: {% include themed-img.html src="/assets/images/screenshots/streaming.png" alt="Streaming response — Wireshark-style frame list" class="some-extra-class" %} `src` should be the *theme-less* legacy filename ending in `.png` — the include rewrites it to `…-dark.png` and `…-light.png`. {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign extra_class = include.class | default: "" -%} {%- assign dark_src = include.src | replace: ".png", "-dark.png" -%} {%- assign light_src = include.src | replace: ".png", "-light.png" -%}
